Drowsiness Detection

Keep the watchers awake.

Overview

What it does

A surveillance network is only as alert as the people watching it. Drowsiness Detection monitors security operators and control-room personnel for signs of fatigue during extended shifts and raises a quiet prompt when attention lapses — directly reducing the single biggest weakness of any 24/7 monitoring operation: the tired human.

It is an inward-facing safeguard rather than a public-surveillance tool, and like expression recognition it is a differentiator that no benchmarked competitor currently matches.

How it works

Capture → detect → act

STEP 01

Monitor

Console-facing cameras track the operator's eye and head state during the shift.

STEP 02

Assess

The model scores fatigue indicators against drowsiness thresholds.

STEP 03

Prompt

A discreet alert reaches the operator and supervisor when attention drops.

Use cases

Where it's deployed

  • 24/7 control rooms. Hold attention through long overnight monitoring shifts.
  • High-camera-count centres. Protect against fatigue when one operator watches many feeds.
  • Critical-event readiness. Keep staff sharp during the windows when response speed matters most.
  • Supervisor oversight. Give shift leads an objective fatigue signal for staffing decisions.
