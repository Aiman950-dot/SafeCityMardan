Perimeter Breach Detection

Overview

What it does

Perimeter Breach Detection lets operators draw virtual boundaries around any secure zone — and raises an instant, location-specific alert the moment an unauthorised person crosses one. It turns a static fence line into an active, intelligent tripwire monitored around the clock.

Boundaries are defined directly on the camera view, so a single deployment can protect dozens of restricted areas simultaneously, each with its own alerting rules.

How it works

Capture → detect → act

STEP 01

Define

Operators draw restricted boundaries and zones on the live camera view.

STEP 02

Watch

The model tracks movement against every defined boundary in real time.

STEP 03

Alert

A crossing triggers an instant, location-tagged breach alert on the dashboard.
