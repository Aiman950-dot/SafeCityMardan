Overview

What it does

A surveillance network's blind spots are its greatest liability. Camera Tamper Detection continuously checks the integrity of every monitored camera — detecting obstruction, rotation, defocusing, or physical interference — and alerts operators the instant a camera is compromised.

By treating the cameras themselves as protected assets, the module ensures that no part of the network can be quietly disabled before an incident without the command centre knowing immediately.

How it works

Capture → detect → act

STEP 01

Baseline

Each camera's normal view and framing are continuously referenced.

STEP 02

Compare

The model detects obstruction, rotation, or defocus against that baseline.

STEP 03

Alert

Any tamper event raises an immediate integrity alert to operators.
