Overview

What it does

Mob & Crowd Density Detection watches the shape and behaviour of crowds — flagging abnormal formations, sudden density surges, and dispersal patterns that indicate a public-order situation is developing. It buys the most valuable thing in crowd management: time to dispatch before disorder escalates.

Rather than counting heads after the fact, the module reads the dynamics of a gathering in real time, giving command staff an early, mapped picture of where pressure is building across the city.

Capture → detect → act

Model

The system classifies normal vs abnormal formation and surge patterns.

STEP 03

Developing situations are mapped and alerted for early responder deployment.
