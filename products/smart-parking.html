Smart Parking Management

Every bay, accounted for.

Automates bay occupancy tracking, flags parking violations, and keeps a timestamped log of all entry and exit events.

Overview

What it does

Smart Parking Management turns ordinary cameras into an automated parking control system — tracking bay-by-bay occupancy, flagging violations, and maintaining a timestamped log of every entry and exit. It removes the need for manual ticketing and gives administrators a live, accurate view of utilisation.

As part of the unified AMS platform, parking data sits alongside security detections on the same command dashboard, so a single deployment serves both public-safety and city-operations needs.

How it works

Capture → detect → act

STEP 01

Map

Parking bays and entry / exit points are mapped onto the camera views.

STEP 02

Track

The system reads occupancy and logs each vehicle entry and exit.

STEP 03

Report

Occupancy, violations and movement logs feed the command dashboard.
